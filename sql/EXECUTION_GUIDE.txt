  CW1_BGT_SALE_Q - CW+1 budget sales
  CW2_BGT_SALE_Q - CW+2 budget sales
  CW3-5_BGT_SALE_Q - CW+3/4/5 budget sales (from future week joins)
  TTL_TRF_OUT_Q - Total transfer out to stores

Purchase Planning:
  BGT_PUR_Q_INIT - Initial budget purchase quantity
  POS_PO_RAISED - Positive PO amount
  NEG_PO_RAISED - Negative PO amount (returns)
  PP_NET_BGT_CF_STK_Q - Net confirmed stock available for planning

Alerts:
  DC_STK_SHORT_Q - DC stock shortage
  DC_STK_EXCESS_Q - DC stock excess
  CO_STK_SHORT_Q - Company (ST+DC) stock shortage
